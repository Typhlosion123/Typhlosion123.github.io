Projects

Some of my best and most impactful projects.

Embarking on my projects to research STEM education and diversity, building a deep learning model to assess DNA bendability using Kmer frequencies, and constructing an FTC robot has been an incredibly important and exhilarating journey. Through my research on STEM education, I aim to uncover ways to make science and technology accessible to a diverse range of students, fostering inclusivity and innovation in the field. Building the deep learning model to explore DNA bendability allows me to delve into the cutting-edge realm of computational biology, unraveling the mysteries of genetic structures. And the thrill of assembling an FTC robot, with its potential to solve real-world challenges, has ignited my passion for engineering and problem-solving. These projects have not only expanded my knowledge and skills but have also reaffirmed the coolness and immense impact that STEM endeavors can have on the world.
