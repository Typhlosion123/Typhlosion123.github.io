Activities

Here are some of my activities outside of school and their impact.

Amidst the whirlwind of academic pursuits, extracurricular activities, and personal growth, my journey as a varsity fencer and captain, as well as a member of the BinaryHearts club, has profoundly shaped my life. The club's mission of donating computers to communities in need and organizing robotics outreach events for lower-income families and children has ignited a sense of purpose within me. Witnessing the transformative impact of technology and education on underprivileged communities has deepened my commitment to philanthropy and community service. Moreover, my involvement as an executive on our local junior board has provided me with invaluable leadership experiences, further fueling my passion for making a positive difference in the world. These endeavors have not only honed my skills but have also inspired me to dedicate my life to creating meaningful change and leaving a lasting impact on those around me.
